My Favorite Pet

Hamster

My favorite pet is the hamster. Why? Because they don't cause too much trouble. They don't get loud and run all over the place like dogs or stare you down and knock objects off the table like cats.

Fun Fact: Hamsters can store food in their cheeks, then eat it later!
