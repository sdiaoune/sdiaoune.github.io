Q&A

What is a hackathon?
A hackathon is a 24-hour event where people get together and build a project (or hack). The hack is not limited in scope, however we ask you to not to start your hack before the hackathon.


Can I go?
In order to go, you have to be a student and over the age of 18.


Is it free?
Yes! Polyhacks is completely FREE to attend! Food and all!


What should I bring?
We recommend that you bring a couple things:
  • Your laptop
  • All your chargers
  • A pillow and blanket (if you want to sleep)
  • If you want to hack some hardware, bring everything you can!
  • Lastly, bring your awesome ideas and passion!


Are there teams?
Absolutely! We strongly encourage being a part of a team. Teams can be anywhere between 1 and 4 people, and you can either come with a team or find one when you get here.
